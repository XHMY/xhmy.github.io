Greetings! My name is Yifan Zeng, currently a graduate student at Oregon State University. I previously held a position as a Research Scientist at a start-up, where I leveraged deep learning methodologies to tackle real-world problems. There, I deal with various challenges including handling insufficient data, low-quality labeling, and domain shift issues. My research focuses primarily on self-supervised learning and foundation models. I am deeply passionate about enhancing the efficiency and reliability of deep learning models, which I try to achieve through designing powerful learning pipelines and knowledge transformation in diverse tasks. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Data Science from Zhuhai College of Jilin University. My GPA is 3.84/4.0, ranking first in the department.
