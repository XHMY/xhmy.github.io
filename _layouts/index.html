Hello! I'm Yifan Zeng, a PhD student in Computer Science at Oregon State University. My current research focuses on the robust planning and reasoning of the large language model (LLM) autonomous system. I previously was a Research Engineer at a start-up, applying deep learning in real-world applications and handling various challenges, such as insufficient data, low-quality labeling, and domain shift. My previous research was primarily on computer vision, medical image analysis, self-supervised learning, and AI for Science. I received my Bachelor's degree in Data Science at Zhuhai College of Jilin University in 2023, achieving a GPA of 3.84/4.0 and ranking first in my department.
