    Yifan Zeng
Hi! I'm Yifan Zeng, a PhD student in Computer Science at Oregon State University, advised by Dr. Huazheng Wang. My current research focuses on the robust planning and reasoning of the large language model (LLM) agentic AI system. I previously was a Research Engineer at a start-up, applying deep learning in real-world applications and handling various challenges. My previous research was primarily on computer vision, medical image analysis, self-supervised learning, and AI for Science. I received my Bachelor's degree in Data Science at Zhuhai College of Jilin University in 2023, achieving a GPA of 3.84/4.0 and ranking first in my department.
